Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I purchase an item?
A. You choose the payment method listed in the top right or use the "buy" button, and provite your InGame name in the notes, along with the item and amount.
Q. How do you receive your items?
A. We will contact you trough the contact information given in the note when completing the chosen purchase method.
Q. How long does the delivery process take?
A. It can range from 5 minutes to 20 minutes, if it takes longer we issue a refund.
